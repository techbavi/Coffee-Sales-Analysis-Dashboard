Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales data from a coffee shop chain to provide insights into business performance. It highlights key metrics such as total sales, total orders, and quantity sold, along with detailed analysis by product, store location, and time. The dashboard helps stakeholders identify sales trends, top-performing products, and customer buying patterns.

Dashboard Features

Month Filter: Select a specific month for analysis

KPIs:

Total Sales

Total Orders

Total Quantity Sold

Month-over-Month comparison

Sales by Date: Daily sales trend with average sales line

Sales by Week: Weekday vs Weekend sales distribution

Sales by Product Category: Sales across categories (Coffee, Tea, Bakery, etc.)

Sales by Product Type: Detailed product-level sales analysis

Sales by Store Location: Location-wise sales with MoM difference

Sales by Day & Hour: Heatmap showing peak sales times

Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales records with the following fields:

transaction_id: Unique ID for each transaction

transaction_date: Date of the transaction

transaction_time: Time of the transaction

transaction_qty: Quantity sold

store_id: Unique store identifier

store_location: Store location

product_id: Unique product identifier

unit_price: Price per unit

product_category: Product category

product_type: Product type within the category
